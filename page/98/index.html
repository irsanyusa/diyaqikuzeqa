Howard B. Cushing (Military Officer)

Jan 1, 1 Full Name: Howard Bass Cushing Profession: Military Officer Biography: Howard B. Cushing was an American officer in the United States Army during the American Civil War and later in the Indian Wars on the western frontier. He was part of the Cushing family, alongside Alonzo Cushing and William B. Cushing. After the Civil War, like many military officers of his time, Cushing continued his service on the western frontier. He became involved in the Indian Wars, a series of conflicts between the United States government and various Native American tribes as the nation expanded westward.
