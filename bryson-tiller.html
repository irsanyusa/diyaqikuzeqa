Bryson Tiller (R&amp;B Singer and Rapper)

Full Name: Bryson Djuan Tiller
Profession: R&B Singer and Rapper

Biography: Bryson Tiller is an American singer, songwriter, and rapper best known for his debut studio album Trapsoul (2015, which peaked at number 8 on the US Billboard 200. His single "Don't" was certified double platinum in the US and was also a top 10 hit on the US Billboard Hot 100.

Tiller began his career in 2011, releasing his debut mixtape titled Killer Instinct Vol.1. He released his second mixtape, titled Killer Instinct Vol.2, in 2013. In 2015, Tiller released his debut studio album titled Trapsoul, which was met with critical and commercial success.

Tiller has since released two additional studio albums, True to Self (2017) and Anniversary (2020). He has also collaborated with artists such as Drake, Rihanna, and Travis Scott. In addition to his music career, Tiller has also appeared in the film The Trap (2020).
