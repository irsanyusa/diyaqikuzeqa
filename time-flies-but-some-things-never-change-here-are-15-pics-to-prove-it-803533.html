Time Flies But Some Things Never Change, Here Are 15 Pics to Prove It

According to Einstein’s Theory of Relativity, time passes faster for your face than for your feet. Yes, time is a mind-blowing phenomenon, and it is inevitable. However, there seem to be things that remain unscathed no matter how many years pass.

Today at Bright Side, we want to present you with 15 heartwarming examples of things that remained true to their core, and will always be that way.

1. “Then and now. She destroys every stuffed animal except this one. :)”

2. “On the left, my husband as a baby with his dad. And on the right, my husband as a dad with our baby.”

3. “First photo of us... 6 years later”

4. “Tried to recreate the photo from the day we brought our pup home a year ago. It did not go as planned.”

5. “One year later”

6. “My sister just got married. Her gift to my parents was to recreate a professional photo that was taken of her when she was 5.”

7. “Our parents are moving from our childhood home — had to recreate this to say goodbye.”

8. “House fire 6 years ago... my kiddo wasn’t scared that night because of this awesome firefighter... look who we just ran into tonight!”

9. “My cat and me 14 years ago, and us today!”

10. “9 years of father and son pictures. Taken in June each year.”

11. “On my cat’s 16th birthday I started the tradition of recreating a photo of us each year on his birthday. His 18th birthday is coming up.”

12. In 2011, he attended Pre-K at the high school. 10 years later he’s a freshman.

13. “My father and I 30 years ago, and then today, my son and myself! ”

14. “At my desk hardly working, 1992-2019”

15. “Recreated our engagement photos 8 years later with the new love of our lives”

Which of these pictures did you find the most heart-warming?
