Miley Cyrus Was Groped By A Fan In Barcelona And It Is So Disturbing

In a video circulating Twitter (which we won't embed here due to its content), Miley and Liam are seen cutting through a mob of fans as they they exit the Mandarin Oriental Hotel and try to make their way to an SUV.

Liam is seen walking ahead of Miley to make a path for her, but a man in the crowd grabs Miley's hair, touches her body, and wraps his arm around her neck to forcibly kiss her. Miley then wriggles ahead of Liam, and when the bodyguards finally realize what's going on, they push the man back into the crowd. Liam wraps his arm around Miley and she walks ahead.

It's extremely disturbing to say the least, and something that many women have unfortunately experienced at least once, whether in a club, at a festival, or even on a crowded sidewalk. It's not okay. Ever. In the past, Miley has dealt with stalkers who tried to invade her home.
