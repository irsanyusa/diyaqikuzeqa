Tolkien orc names are based on what language?

The names for Tolkien's orcs are primarily based on two languages: the Black Speech and the Common Speech (Westron).

What does gro mean in orc names?

In orc names, "gro" is often used as a suffix or prefix to convey a particular meaning or characteristic associated with the orc. It can be associated with strength or power, indicating that the orc is strong or formidable. For example, the name "Grom" signifies strength.
