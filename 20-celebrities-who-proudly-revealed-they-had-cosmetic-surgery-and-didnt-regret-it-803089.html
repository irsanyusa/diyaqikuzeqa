20 Celebrities Who Proudly Revealed They Had Cosmetic Surgery and Didnt Regret It

Modern plastic surgery was born in 1814. Today plastic surgery and procedures have come a long way and have become a beauty aid. It’s natural for celebrities to get a nip or tuck here or there, considering their need to look their best. Of course, this doesn’t mean that everyone does it. And even for the ones who get something done, not everyone will admit it.

This is why Bright Side admires these bold celebrities who spoke out about their plastic surgery experience. These gorgeous women not only take pride in their decision but also inspire others to be body positive, in their own way.

1. Dolly Parton

Singer, actress, and overall do-gooder, Dolly Parton is 75 and still a stunner. She has been quoted, famously, for saying “If something is bagging, sagging, or dragging, I’ll tuck it, suck it, or pluck it.”

2. Katy Perry

Pop music bestseller, 36-year-old Katy Perry, is now a mother and engaged to Orlando Bloom. She’s happy to speak about the laser treatments she’s done for her skin and the undereye fillers she’s gotten to fix her undereye circles. Other than that, Perry claims to be all real.

3. Lisa Kudrow

Lisa Kudrow, as in Phoebe Buffay from the hit sitcom Friends, is making 58 look good. She admitted in an interview to getting a nose job when she was in high school. Kudrow said that, in her mind, it made her go from “hideous to not hideous.”

4. Vanessa Williams

Vanessa Williams lit up the screen dancing with Chayanne in 1998’s Dance With Me. The singer, actress, and former Miss America is 58 years old now and is still glowing. She has admitted to trying various procedures like micro-needling but calls Botox her regimen.

5. Cindy Crawford

Supermodel Cindy Crawford is a very well-maintained 55 years old today. She’s admitted to having some cosmetic procedures done and says that she never judges anyone who has had any procedure. Mostly because it’s a personal choice.

6. Kourtney Kardashian

Kourtney Kardashian, the oldest of all the Kardashian-Jenner sisters is gorgeous at 42, and a mother of 3. She has openly admitted to getting breast implants and claims it’s no secret.

7. Cardi B

Belcalis Marlenis Almánzar, known to the world as Cardi B, has risen to be the foremost name in female rap stars, all at the age of 28. With an eclectic sense of style, Cardi B once admitted in an interview that she’s gone for injections to increase her behind (for a better shape).

8. Jane Fonda

Jane Fonda, actress and activist is 83 years old now. She is outspoken when it comes to admitting to the various cosmetic procedures she’s had done and she has mentioned the same in her blog.

9. Olivia Colman

Olivia Colman, who is all of 47, is the British actress who portrays Queen Elizabeth II in the Netflix hit series, The Crown. She unabashedly admitted to getting Botox and loved the refreshed complexion it gave her. Incidentally, so did her husband.

10. Ashley Tisdale

High School Musical star Ashley Tisdale went public in 2007 with the news that she had rhinoplasty to fix her nose, for health reasons. Today, this mom of one is 36 and is happy with her decision even though, back then, she was judged for it.

11. Patricia Heaton

Patricia Heaton is best known for playing Debra Barone in Everybody Loves Raymond. Today, she’s a gorgeous 63, and back in 2003, she came clean about plastic surgery. She went in for a tummy tuck and breast reduction. Fitting into gowns again has made her happy.

12. Kelly Rowland

Kelly Rowland glows at 40, and this former Destiny’s Child band member admitted to getting breast augmentation surgery. Happy with the results, she hinted that she may be getting another one in the future as well.

13. Angelina Jolie

Actress and humanitarian, Angelina Jolie carries her 46 years very well. When she was diagnosed with a high risk of getting breast cancer, genetically, Jolie made the bold decision of getting a preventive double mastectomy. It was followed up by breast reconstruction surgery with implants and she is happy with her decision.

14. Linda Evangelista

Supermodel Linda Evangelista is a gorgeous 56 today and is unafraid to talk about the bevy of cosmetic procedures she has had, including lasers, fillers, and Botox. She says that while she is not trying to look younger, she does want to look good.

15. Kylie Jenner

Kylie Jenner, 24, has been labeled the most influential celebrity in fashion and has made a name for herself as a businesswoman as well. She denies going under the knife but happily admits to having fillers when needed.

16. Kelly Ripa

50-year-old Kelly Ripa has a smile that can light up a room. But even she’s not averse to getting a little Botox, especially when people assumed she was angry because of her forehead. She also gets Botox to avoid sweaty armpits.

17. Brooke Shields

From model to actress, Brooke Shields, 56, has been the dream girl of many. She has tried Botox in the past and today she believes in a non-invasive cosmetic procedure called WarmSculpting.

18. Anna Faris

Anna Faris, the comedy queen from movies like the Scary Movie franchise is a stunning 44 today. She has admitted to getting lip fillers and breast augmentation done to look better.

19. Ariel Winter

23-year-old Ariel Winter is often talked about for having cosmetic surgery done, but without proof. That said, the Modern Family star has admitted to getting a breast reduction surgery done, mostly because she felt uncomfortable with her body.

20. Sharon Osbourne

Sharon Osbourne is vivacious and, at 68, quite the looker. And she’s open about the fact that a lot of her refreshed looks can be attributed to procedures, including the fourth facelift in 2020.
