Competitive eater Matt Stonie Eats 5 pound burrito in 2 minutes

May 11, 2015 2:48 PM EDT

In this impressive and disgusting clip, competitive eater Matt “Megatoad” Stonie polishes off the largest burrito we have ever seen in two minutes flat. And when we say large, we really mean large. Stonie says the burrito is 18 inches long, weighing in at five pounds. He calls it Burritozilla, which seems fitting.

After watching Stonie calmly devour the entire thing, you’ll see why he’s the #2 ranked competitive eater in the world. Oh, and you’ll also feel positively dainty the next time you eat a Chipotle burrito, which is truly nothing compared to this monstrosity.
