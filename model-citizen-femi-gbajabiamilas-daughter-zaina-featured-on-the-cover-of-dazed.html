Femi Gbajabiamila's daughter, Zaina, featured on the cover of Dazed magazine

Zaina poses on the cover of Dazed's commemorative issue celebrating the design legend that is Vivienne Westwood and Britain's bright young things.

Its only right that the daughter of the oh so stylish Femi and Sal Gbajabiamila would follow in her parents footsteps. Zaina, a model signed to Next models in London, has now been featured front and center on the cover of Dazed magazines Summer issue celebrating the characters making Britain great and the fashion anarchist herself, Vivienne Westwood.
