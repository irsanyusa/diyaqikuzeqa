10 Light-Hearted Comedies Thatll Cheer You Right Up

After a long stressful day, we all look for something that will help us relax, and one of the options is to watch a comedy movie. That is when we end up spending hours searching and reading countless reviews to choose the right one. But, there are comedy movies that you’ve probably never thought could hold the power to put a smile on your face.

Bright Side shares a collection of comedy movies that are unexpectedly thrilling, entertaining, and heart-warming.

1. Isn’t It Romantic (2019)

Isn’t It Romantic is a romantic-comedy that makes fun of all other rom-coms. This makes it perfect for everyone, even if you are not into these types of comedies. Rebel Wilson brings a lot of energy, and is relatable and witty. She’ll fill you without hope, positive vibes, and laughter.

2. We’re the Millers (2013)

This is not an ordinary family trip comedy movie. Some neighbors go on a journey to cross the border as a fake family, just to transfer some products to the mafia. It’s filled with action and obstacles that the “family” needs to overcome. Who would have known that Jennifer Aniston would be so great for the character she plays?

3. The Lovebirds (2020)

It is never a good idea to let a stranger into your car, even if he poses as a police officer. Even worse, running from the scene of the crime. These 2 lovebirds find themselves in a situation that they clearly can’t handle. But the amusing part is them trying to solve the issue all by themselves when they don’t have any idea what they are doing. The Lovebirds will be out on the 22nd of May 2020 in the US.

4.This Means War (2012)

2 best friends, one girl. Taking every step carefully to not destroy their relationship. But, who will she choose? This comedy is filled with some romance and a lot of action. It will definitely cheer you up.

5. A Thousand Words (2012)

Eddie Murphy is known for his stunning comedy movies, and this is no exception. This one has just one different twist. When a magical tree appears in front of his house, everything changes. The talkative and successful man needs to choose his words and spend them wisely. It will definitely entertain you and at the same time, teach you a life lesson.

6. The Change-Up (2011)

A toxic bachelor and a family man are 2 best friends who end up changing their lives. Once the change happens, the real entertainment starts. They both have different opinions about their new lives and the way they should make decisions.

7. Tooth Fairy (2010)

It’s time to watch The Rock in a family — comedy movie. Yup, in a totally different character and it’s worth it. The meanest hockey player, who doesn’t believe in fairy tales, becomes part of that world. So, the tests start, and he will find his own special way to finish his responsibilities as a tooth fairy.

8. Long Shot (2019)

A political spin in a romantic comedy at first seems unreal. However, this movie breaks all the rules and you will end up actually enjoying it. Long forgotten best friends with 2 different backgrounds and careers meet in the most unexpected way. They start a journey that they will never forget. After all, it is not easy to hold on to your grace as the secretary of state when you are funny and weird at heart.

9. Always Be My Maybe (2019)

Sasha is a celebrity chef with a bright future ahead of her. Everything seems perfect until her fiancé breaks up with her over the phone. And then her first crush shows up at her new home. Sometimes it’s not that bad to have a history with someone and have feelings for them. But, it’s not easy when Sasha is blind to that fact. She just doesn’t’ want to start dating all over again, so all of her dates take a wrong turn. Who will be the lucky man?

10. Second Act (2018)

It is never too late to start a career. Well, that is what Maya (Jennifer Lopez) tells us in this movie. But, starting a new chapter in your life doesn’t mean forgetting about the people who were always standing by your side. Jennifer Lopez really knows how to touch our hearts, even when acting in a comedy.

What is your favorite comedy movie? Which one will you watch first from this list?
