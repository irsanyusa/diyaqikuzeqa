Dr. Pimple Popper Just Popped An Exploding Cyst In Slow Motion In New Instagram Video

Dr. Pimple Popper downplayed the excitement in her first Instagram video with the caption, "Just a little pop for you here..." She also added, "oh and a little splash mask action! #drpimplepopper"

The video starts with a close-up view of the cyst located on her patient's arm. It is lurking under the surface and it's hard to tell how big it really is. Dr. Pimple Popper dives right in and starts squeezing. She adds a bit of pressure and boom, out comes a blob of dark goo. It shoots out of the cyst and straight toward the doctor. She needs more than a splash mask to protect her from that.

This particular video is silent, so Dr. Pimple Popper doesn't add her reaction. She does, however, add a slow motion replay to ensure all her viewers saw every last messy bit from the best angle.

Dr. Pimple Popper's fans are seriously impressed by this squirting cyst. One commenter shared, "All over the jumper!". The contents landed squarely on Dr. Pimple Popper. Another fan shared, "Gonna need clean scrubs after that one." Another Popaholic said, "I was waiting for one of those explosives !!" It certainly was explosive.

That's not all Dr. Pimple Popper has in store for her fans. Her next video is a classic cyst removal. She captioned this pop: "Getting a little cheeky out here! Happy weekend, TAG someone youre hanging out with this weekend! #drpimplepopper" Her patient has a cyst on her cheek that looks like it is ready to burst. Thankfully, Dr. Pimple Popper is here to take care of it safely and swiftly.

She creates an opening over the cyst and squeezes gently. Dr. Pimple Popper says she's "squeezing the cheek like grandma does". That sweet technique does the trick. The contents of the cyst come spewing out. One Popaholic commented, "That looks like oatmeal or rice." It sure does, but the best part is yet to come.

Dr. Pimple Popper goes back in with her tweezers and removes the cyst sac. It peels off in one big piece. Talk about satisfying pops.
