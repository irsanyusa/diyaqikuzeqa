Alana “Honey Boo Boo” Thompson recently took to TikTok to shoot down rumors that she’s pregnant at age 17. The reality star also just attended her high school prom and shared video of her glam look on social media.

Alana ‘Honey Boo Boo’ Thompson Denies Being Pregnant

According to The Sun, Alana went live on TikTok over the weekend to address rumors that she and her boyfriend Dralin Carswell are expecting a child together. As Alana said, “The dumbest s**t comes out y’all’s mouth … Like, no I’m not pregnant! I haven’t even graduated high school, yet, okay?”

Alana went on to say that she wants to graduate high school and attend college “before I even think about having kids.” The star’s relationship with Carswell has raised some eyebrows since they went public in 2021, due to their age gap.

During her live video, Alana also reportedly responded to people who claim she uses a “blaccent,” saying, “No I don’t speak ghetto, no I don’t think I’m Black. Y’all are literally on my live every f**king day so pressed about the way I talk. It really pisses you off that bad the way I talk, how I speak?”

The Reality Star Recently Attended Her Prom

TMZ reports that Alana attended her high school prom on Sunday in Georgia. The outlet shared photos of the star posing in a long pink dress beside Carswell and her mom, June Shannon. Alana reportedly pulled up to the dance in a horse-drawn carriage.

Alana also shared some videos of her glam hair and makeup look on social media. She wore her long blonde hair half up in a high ponytail and showed off some seriously long fingernails, as well as some bright pink eye shadow.

TMZ also points out that just last month, Carswell was arrested for DUI following a three-mile police chase with Alana in the car. Alana wasn’t taken into custody, and was instead picked up by her sister Pumpkin.
