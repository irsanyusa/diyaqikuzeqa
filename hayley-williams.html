Hayley Williams (Pop Singer and Rocker)

Full Name: Hayley Nichole Williams
Profession: Pop Singer and Rocker

Biography: Hayley Williams is an American singer-songwriter. She is best known as the lead vocalist and primary songwriter of the rock band Paramore. Williams rose to fame in 2004 after the release of Paramore's debut album All We Know Is Falling, which featured hit singles like "Pressure" and "Emergency."

Williams has since released five studio albums with Paramore. She has been nominated for four Grammy Awards, winning one in 2018 for Best Rock Song for the single "Ain't It Fun." She also featured on B.o.B.'s hit single "Airplanes" (2010) which peaked at number 2 on the Billboard Hot 100 in the United States.
